Our Story

Born from a Family's Love

The I AM Memory Book Series began when Maria Darling-Jones watched her father navigate the challenges of dementia. She created something simple but powerful: a coloring book filled with the sights, sounds, and traditions of his Greek Cypriot heritage.

Each book is bilingual, written in both English and the native language of the culture it represents. Pages feature gentle prompts like "I like to eat ___" and "I like to go to ___" alongside beautiful line drawings of culturally familiar scenes, encouraging conversation, memory recall, and the simple joy of colouring.

When her father sat down with the book, the results were remarkable. He smiled, he talked, he remembered. What started as a family's solution is now a growing series celebrating cultures around the world.

How It Works

1

Choose Their Culture

Pick the book that matches your loved one's heritage. Greek Cypriot, English, Italian, French, American — each edition is written in English and the native language.

2

Colour, Talk & Remember

Sit together as they colour familiar scenes. Fill-in-the-blank prompts like "I like to eat ___" spark conversation and invite them to share their own memories.

3

Watch Them Light Up

As colours fill the page, something beautiful happens. Stories return, smiles appear, and for those precious moments, your loved one is fully present.

Frequently Asked Questions

I AM Memory Books are bilingual coloring books designed specifically for people living with dementia. Each book celebrates a different cultural heritage with familiar scenes, gentle prompts like "I like to eat ___", and simple line drawings perfect for coloring. They encourage conversation, memory recall, and meaningful connection between caregivers and loved ones.

The series currently includes Greek Cypriot, English, Italian, French, and American editions, each available in both Grandfather and Grandmother versions — 10 books in total. More cultures including Irish, Indian, Spanish, and Chinese are being added regularly.
